PhDRank ranks doctoral programs by where graduates land — faculty, postdocs, industry — traced to verified public career records via ORCID, CSRankings, and econphdplacements.com. If a destination cannot be confirmed, it is excluded from scoring.

Each program receives a composite score normalized within its field over a rolling 10-year window.
